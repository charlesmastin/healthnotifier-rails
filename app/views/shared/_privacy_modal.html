About Privacy

Use the privacy control for each component of your LifeSticker to manage its visibility when scanned or viewed by members of your LifeCircle.

peopleHealth Network Visible to registered users who scan your LifeSticker.
verified_userAuthorized Viewers Visible to verified health care providers or select users you authorize.
lock Private Visible only to you.

Registered users may scan your LifeSticker and view details for a short time, but only those whom you add to your LifeCircle will have continued access.
